About Us

MovieAtlas is a movie streaming-style website that showcases different genres such as Action, Horror, Comedy, Drama, and Romance. Users can explore movies, view details, and watch trailers.

This project was created as part of a school requirement to demonstrate skills in HTML and CSS. It focuses on building a responsive and visually appealing interface inspired by streaming platforms.
